==============================================================
KEY INSIGHTS
==============================================================

1. Email is the efficiency powerhouse. At 15.16x ROAS, email marketing
   delivers the highest return of any channel. The segmented nurture
   sequences introduced in February were a turning point — they should
   be expanded to cover additional lifecycle stages.

2. LinkedIn needs strategic reassessment. At 0.86x ROAS, LinkedIn is
   operating at a loss on last-touch attribution. However, pipeline data
   suggests it influences larger enterprise deals. A shift to pipeline-
   weighted attribution and ABM-focused campaigns may better capture
   its true value.

3. Google Ads branded search is carrying performance. The 7.3x ROAS on
   branded terms inflates overall Google Ads numbers. Non-brand campaigns
   average only 2.1x ROAS and need continued optimization, particularly
   around landing page experience.

4. Rising Meta CPMs are a warning sign. March CPMs increased 18%
   month-over-month. If this trend continues into Q2, Facebook spend
   should be reallocated toward higher-performing retargeting audiences
   and away from broad prospecting.

5. SEO content is building a compounding asset. The 31% QoQ growth in
   organic sessions with minimal spend increase demonstrates the flywheel
   effect. The GA4 + BigQuery guide alone is generating an estimated
   15-20 demo requests per month at near-zero marginal cost.

==============================================================
RECOMMENDATIONS FOR Q2 2026
==============================================================

1. INCREASE EMAIL INVESTMENT (+$1,500/month)
   Expand the segmented nurture program to include onboarding sequences,
   feature adoption campaigns, and upsell flows. Projected incremental
   ROAS: 10-12x. Priority: HIGH.

2. RESTRUCTURE LINKEDIN STRATEGY
   Reduce LinkedIn spend by 30% ($2,430/month savings). Shift remaining
   budget toward Account-Based Marketing (ABM) campaigns targeting named
   enterprise accounts. Implement pipeline-weighted attribution to measure
   true influence. Test LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms as an alternative to
   landing page conversions. Priority: HIGH.

3. OPTIMIZE GOOGLE ADS NON-BRAND CAMPAIGNS
   Conduct landing page A/B tests for top 10 non-brand keywords. Implement
   Performance Max campaigns for mid-funnel audiences. Target: improve
   non-brand ROAS from 2.1x to 3.0x by end of Q2. Priority: MEDIUM.

4. DOUBLE DOWN ON SEO CONTENT
   Commission 8 new long-form guides targeting high-intent keywords
   identified in Q1 keyword research. Focus on comparison and integration
   topics where we have product authority. Estimated 6-month traffic
   impact: +25,000 monthly organic sessions. Priority: MEDIUM.
